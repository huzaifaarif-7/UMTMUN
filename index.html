About UMT MUN

UMT MUN is the official Model United Nations conference hosted by the University of Management and Technology. It brings together passionate delegates from across the nation to debate pressing global issues, develop diplomatic skills, and foster youth leadership through dynamic committee sessions and cultural exchange.
